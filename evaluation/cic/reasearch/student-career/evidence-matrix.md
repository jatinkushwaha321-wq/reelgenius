# Overall Conclusion

Current evidence suggests the creators share a common educational philosophy centred around helping ambitious young people build modern careers through execution, skill acquisition and opportunity creation.

However, there are strong signals that Tharun Naik and Ishan Sharma represent two neighbouring expressions of this philosophy rather than identical creator identities.

A third creator is required before freezing the Creator DNA.
